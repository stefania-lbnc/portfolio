hey there!

I’m a creative designer with a passion for crafting everything from magazines to advertising materials for all sorts of clients. Recently, I had the chance to give a fresh, modern look to the Egyptian Academy’s magazine in Rome, making it visually striking and more engaging for readers.

I’m especially drawn to UX/UI design creating intuitive, user-friendly experiences is where my heart is. With a degree in industrial design from La Sapienza University, I’m ready to take on all kinds of design challenges. My style leans towards clean, modern layouts that balance beauty and function; every detail should serve a purpose while looking great.

I’m always up for a new project and love finding ways to keep growing in my work. Collaboration is key for me, I truly enjoy working with clients to bring their vision to life. Ready to make something amazing together?
