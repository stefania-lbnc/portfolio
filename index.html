I’m a Visual Designer working across editorial, advertising, and digital design. My work focuses on clarity, balance, and thoughtful visual communication.

Recently, I redesigned the magazine of the Egyptian Academy in Rome, giving it a fresh and more contemporary visual identity. I’m particularly interested in UX/UI design, where visual structure and functionality come together to shape intuitive experiences.

I hold a degree in Industrial Design from La Sapienza University in Rome and enjoy exploring different design challenges, always aiming for simple, effective solutions.
