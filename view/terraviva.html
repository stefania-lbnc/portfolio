TERRAVIVA _Logo Design

The main idea from which this logo has been created is based on TerraViva's philosophy, which is based on promoting innovative practices focused on environmental and social sustainability.

In the work process, a lateral idea has been sought, always thinking outside the box.

The logo proposed for TerraViva goes beyond simple graphic representation; It is about embodying the fundamental values ​​of sustainability, innovation and aesthetic beauty. The choice of a butterfly made up of golden triangles results in a visually striking and conceptually profound emblem.

The butterfly, a universal symbol of transformation and renewal, merges with the geometric precision of golden triangles and geometric shapes, representing the harmonious fusion between natural beauty and mathematical accuracy. This combination symbolizes the company's commitment to sustainability, showing the evolution towards an architectural design that balances innovation, aesthetics and respect for the environment.

In this way we move away from the classic idea of ​​the leaf or flower to represent sustainability in an urban environment, a concept reflected in the union of the wings of the design.

Thus, the logo not only becomes a visual representation, but also a symbol of the company's ideals: the union between creativity, precision and sustainability in the world of architecture.
