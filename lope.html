UX/UI Design - Case studies

We aim to create a community where users can communicate

Main Problem

You only needed an app like this to pre-order the album; after that, the app is useless.


Our Goal

We aim to create a community where users can communicate about the band inside the app. In this way, they can keep using the app.


Concept Design

The general idea is to have a page with very few elements. Although the main components should be more visible, I would like the album cover to be featured as a full background whenever possible, maintaining the visual identity of the band and reinforcing the brand's image.

FINDINGS

1. Most usability study participants need help finding the product they want because of the page organization.

2. Most usability study participants prefer to recheck their data before confirming the purchase.

3. They also prefer to have a complete recall of their purchase during the checkout.
