Google Ux Design Certificate at Coursera We aim to create a community where users can communicate Main Problem | You only needed an app like this to pre-order the album; after that, the app is useless.
Our Goal | We aim to create a community where users can communicate about the band inside the app. In this way, they can keep using the app. Concept Design | The general idea is to have a page with very few elements. Although the main components should be more visible, I would like the album cover to be featured as a full background whenever possible, maintaining the visual identity of the band and reinforcing the brand's image.
